# Diamonds

Given a list of numbers, say [1, 3, 5, 7], find all ratios

<pre class="narrow">
    1       3       5       7

1   1/1     3/1     5/1     7/1

3   1/3     3/3     5/3     7/3

5   1/5     3/5     5/5     7/5

7   1/7     3/7     5/7     7/7
</pre>

and reduce within one octave

<pre class="narrow">
    1       3       5       7

1   1/1     3/2     5/4     7/4

3   4/3     1/1     5/3     7/6

5   8/5     6/5     1/1     7/5

7   8/7    12/7    10/7     1/1
</pre>

The unique ratios 1/1, 8/7, 7/6, 6/5, 5/4, 4/3, 7/5, 10/7, 3/2, 8/5, 5/3, 12/7,
7/4 form the [1, 3, 5, 7] diamond.
